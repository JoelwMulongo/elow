About Us

Elow college is an institution that majors in the proficiencies of general English. We have witnessed the immense growth of the school from its founding time to date.

We have had the chance to render services in various departments of the school first as a teacher and also administrative duties. As a teacher, we positioned the IELTS department, where we have handled the section since the establishment of the school. The school is equipped with materials to enable a smooth learning process.
