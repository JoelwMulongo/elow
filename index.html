Elow college of professional studies

A tertiary institution offering both Certificate and Diploma courses under Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority (TVET) guidelines.

We train English proficiencies such as GMAT, TOEFL, IELTS, GRE, OET, SAT and other languages; French, Swahili and German.
We've been teaching students from different cultural backgrounds and countries such as Somalia, Sudan, Egypt, Yemen, Chad and Tanzania. Due to this we respect and appreciate all students with diverse cultures.
Elow is equipped with materials to enable a smooth learning process and success of students


Our Aim Is Your Success we provide both Face-To-Face or Online Courses

We welcome all those who would want to further their studies in relation to languages and also those who are planning to travel abroad either for work or studies to enroll with us. You are assured of success and a platform to enhance your skills as well as develop new ideas. As a school, we are committed to serve you. Thank you.
